About Me

Hi, I'm Rongxin, and welcome!
I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Feinberg School of Medicine, Northwestern University. Prior to this, I received my Ph.D. from the State Key Laboratory of Digital Medical Engineering at Southeast University, during which I did part of my Ph.D. training in Dr. Jean-Louis Mergny's lab at Ã‰cole Polytechnique in France.

Moving forward, and with the goal of unlocking the secrets hidden within the "Book of Heaven" (the human genome), my research interests lie in the analysis and interpretation of large-scale human multi-omics data through bioinformatics approaches.
